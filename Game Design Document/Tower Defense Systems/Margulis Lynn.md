# Margulis Lynn

## 1. Description

- Use as a basic damage dealer unit
- Use telekinesis made with electromagnets and wave transmitters to damage the first enemy in its range
- Powered by Fluxite

## 2. Attack Pattern

- O > > >

## 3. Attachments

- Voltage Buffer: High speed - low damage
	- Cost: 1500 TS
	- Attack speed: $\times 4$
	- Follows Ohm’s Laws
- Ampe Buffer: High damage - low speed
	- Cost: 1000 TS
	- Attack speed: $\div 4$
	- Follows Ohm’s Laws
- Smoothbore Transmitter:
	- Cost: 4000 TS
	- Critical chance: 30%
	- Critical damage: x2
	- Miss chance: 20%
	- Transmitting using raw power without stabilizing, trade off by accuracy

- Health: 360 HP

- Damage: 60 DPS/s
- Projectile per hit: 3 p
