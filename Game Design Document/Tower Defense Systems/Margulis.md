# Margulis

## 1. Description

- Use as a basic damage dealer unit
- Use telekinesis made with electromagnets and wave transmitters to damage the first enemy in its range
- Powered by Fluxite

## 2. Attack Pattern

- O > > >

## 3. Attachments

- Destructive Interference:
	- Cost: 3000 TS
	- Damage: 5 DPS/s
	- Chill chance: 50%
	- Chill duration: 1 s
	- Movement slowed down rate: 40%
	- Transmitter generates 2 opposite unstable waves, collision expands the space around it, slowing enemies hit

## 5. Statistics

- Health: 280
- Defense: 40 DEF
- Damage: 20 DPS/s
- Attack speed: 1 hit/s
- Cooldown: 4 s
