-----

Idea & Scope
Genre: Tower defense + Automation, Level-based
Art style: Techy, sci-fi
Gameplay needs:
- Satisfy constant combat and drama for tower defense players
- While still maintaining reasonable breaks for automation players
- Mechanics support complex material processing for automation players
Platform targets: Windows
Technical constraints:
- CPU based rendering
- Low memory and processing performance

-----

Game design document

Gameplay loop:
- Level-based, sequential order with extra sidequests
- The board is a grid
- Enemies move with the y and x axis
- Gameplay heavily relies on problem-solving
- Phase 1: You start by building a factory
- After phase 1:
	- The enemies start spawning after some time
	- At this point, the factory starts operating
	- And the player can also extend their factory or building towers
- Phase 2 have 2 modes alternatively: charge and haste:
	- Charge mode is slow, the player can collect resources during this tie
	- Haste mode is dramatic, organized attacks aimed to destroy the player's buildings.

Mines:
- Contains resources
- Medium health

Storage:
- Automatically collect Mines in a certain radius
- Storage can transport its items in 4 directions
- Costs more than Belts
- High health

Belts:
- Belts can transport its items from 1 direction to the opposite
- Medium health

Hall:
- Materials stored inside the hall can be used to build Buildings
- Very high health

Towers:
- Towers can be bought by the materials gathered by Hall
	- Towers have buying cooldown
- Towers can be placed on belts
- Towers can, includes but not limited to, attack, heal itself and buff other Towers:
	- Towers have 2 tiers, which has special effects and buffs
- Towers can be merged to create another type of Tower with a cost of material (POTENTIAL IDEA, NOT PLANNED YET)
	- Merged towers of the same type will have summed tiers
	- Merged towers of different types will have max tiers of the towers

Lootboxes:
- Contains Coins which can be used to buy in Shop
- Or contains Usables which can be activated manually by the player
- Drop as a random or set Event

Usables:
- Usables are tools the player can use to manipulate the game field

Delivery methods:
- All devivery methods have a range attribute to scale effective range
- Axis:
	- Fire projects in a set axis
- Lob:
	- Fire projectiles into the air and drop it on the ground in a donut radius
	- Have hang time
	- Have inaccuracy to encourage the use of Axis and Salvo
- Salvo:
	- Fire projectiles in the position that the player commands
	- Range is restricted in specific shapes
	- Has a charge up animation when the player select a position so they have to plan up
- Jack:
	- Moves in a direction set by the player every few ticks
	- Bounces off contact with wall
	- Deals effects to entities in a 3x3 area around the tower when move
	- Can connect to each other

Tower classes:
- Swight:
	- Deals damage
- Charge:
	- Deals knockback or stun
- Engine:
	- Increase firerate and damage of ally towers
- Drup:
	- Retrigger the effects of affected ally towers
	- Subclasses:
		- Echo:
			- Delivery: Jack
			- Effect: Leaves a trail for 1s that retriggers ally towers in a 3x3 range once after the trail ends

Towers:
- Rifle:
	- Class: Swight
	- Low firerate
	- High damage
	- Target strongest enemy
- Cluster:
	- Class: Swight
	- On impact, split into 3 smaller projectiles that scatters out
	- Low damage
	- Low firerate
	- Target weakest enemy
- Gravnet:
	- Class: Charge
	- Inspiration: Netted melon
	- Delivery: Axis or Salvo
	- Fire projectiles that, on impact, pulls enemies towards the center of the impact
	- High firerate
	- Low damage
- Grapeshot:
	- Class: Charge
	- Inspiration: Grape
	- Delivery: Axis or Salvo
	- Fire projectiles that, on impact, pushes enemies away from the center of the impact
	- Medium firerate
	- High damage

Walls:
- Walnut:
	- 1-tile wall
	- Medium cooldown
	- High health
- Pealet:
	- A 3-tile wall
	- Medium cooldown
	- Medium health

Enemies classes:
- Sickle:
	- Target Hall
- Swarm:
	- Fast
	- Low health
	- Comes in group
- Siege:
	- Target Walls
	- Subclasses:
		- Ram:
			- High health
			- Low movement
		- Ballista:
			- Immovable
			- High attack speed
		- Rambalist:
			- Has stats of both Ram and Ballista, although for movement it's Immovable
			- Short range
			- Extra damage to Walls and Hall

Map elements:
- Chasms:
	- Jack towers can't move over them
	- Ground enemies can't move over them
	- Lob projectiles can move over them
	- Air enemies can walk over them
